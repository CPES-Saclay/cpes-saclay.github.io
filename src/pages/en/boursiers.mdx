# Scholarships and financial aid

The CPES supports students in accessing financial aid schemes (needs-based scholarships, emergency grants, housing allowances). The information below gives an overview of the procedures and useful contacts.

## Scholarships and criteria

- National scholarships are awarded according to CROUS criteria via Parcoursup or online application when required.
- Specific grants may be offered on an occasional basis by partner institutions or foundations.

## Additional financial support

- Housing allowance (APL/ALS): contact the CROUS and prepare the required supporting documents.
- Mobility or emergency grants: contact the school's social services or the academic coordination team.

## Procedures and contacts

To put together an application or ask questions:
- Social services at the Lycée International de Palaiseau (internal contact details).
- CPES academic coordination team.

Remember to submit your applications early (allow for processing times) and keep all required supporting documents.
