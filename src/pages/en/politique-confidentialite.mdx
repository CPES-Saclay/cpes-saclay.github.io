# Privacy policy

**Last updated**: 6 May 2026

## Data controller

The controller of the personal data collected via this site is: Sarra DAMAK.

Data Protection Officer (DPO): dpo@cpes-paris-saclay.fr

## Data collected and purposes

This site uses **PostHog** (instance hosted in the European Union) in cookieless mode, for the sole purpose of **audience measurement**. No tracking cookies are placed on this basis, and no consent is required for this measurement in accordance with the GDPR and French CNIL guideline no. 2020-091.

**Legal basis**: legitimate interest of the data controller in measuring and improving the site's traffic (art. 6.1.f GDPR).

**Processor**: PostHog, through its European instance (`eu.i.posthog.com`), acts as a processor within the meaning of article 28 GDPR.

**Retention period**: data collected via PostHog are kept for a period not exceeding the limits set by the applicable regulations on audience measurement.

**Transfers outside the EU**: no data is transferred outside the European Union in the context of audience measurement (EU PostHog instance).

## Embedded content

Content embedded via **iframe** (YouTube, LinkedIn) is subject to the user's explicit consent. These iframes are blocked by default and only load after acceptance. Their activation may involve the placement of third-party cookies and transfers of data to the United States, governed by the privacy policies of their respective publishers.

## Your rights

In accordance with articles 15 to 21 GDPR, you have at all times the following rights over your personal data:
- Right of access,
- Right to rectification,
- Right to erasure,
- Right to restriction of processing,
- Right to object,
- Right to portability.

To exercise these rights, you may contact the data controller or the DPO (contact details above).

**Due to the cookieless mode (no persistent identifier), the effective exercise of these rights may be technically limited: the data collected does not, in practice, allow a request to be linked to an individual user.**
