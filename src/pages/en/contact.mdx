# Contact

## Administration of the Lycée International de Palaiseau Paris-Saclay

LIPPS is in charge of enrolments and the administration of the programme. You can contact LIPPS through [the official website](https://www.lipsp.fr/).

Administrative contact:
contact-cpes@universite-paris-saclay.fr  
Academic contact:
cpes.0912433a@ac-versailles.fr

## Website team

This website is maintained by students from all backgrounds of the programme in collaboration with the CPES academic team. For any question about the website, you can open an issue or a discussion on [our Github](https://github.com/CPES-Saclay) or send us an email at the following address: [site@cpes-paris-saclay.fr](mailto:site@cpes-paris-saclay.fr)

## Student union (BDE)

For any question about student life, you can also contact the BDE CPES Paris-Saclay by email at [bde@cpes-paris-saclay.fr](mailto:bde@cpes-paris-saclay.fr) or on Linkedin [BDE CPES Paris-Saclay](https://www.linkedin.com/company/bde-cpes-paris-saclay).
