# Contact

## Administration of the Lycée International de Palaiseau Paris-Saclay

LIPPS is in charge of enrolments and the administration of the programme. You can contact LIPPS through [the official website](https://www.lipsp.fr/).

Contact for administrative questions:
contact-cpes@universite-paris-saclay.fr  
Contact for the academic coordinator:
cpes.0912433a@ac-versailles.fr

## Website team

This website is maintained by students from all backgrounds of the programme. For any question about the website, you can open an issue or a discussion on [our Github](https://github.com/CPES-Saclay).

## Student union (BDE)

For any question about student life, you can also contact the BDE CPES Paris-Saclay by email at [cpesparissaclay@gmail.com](mailto:cpesparissaclay@gmail.com) or on Linkedin [BDE CPES Paris-Saclay](https://www.linkedin.com/company/bde-cpes-paris-saclay).
