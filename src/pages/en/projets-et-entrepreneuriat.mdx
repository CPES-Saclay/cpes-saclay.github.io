# Projects and entrepreneurship

The CPES strongly encourages initiative and entrepreneurship. From the first year, students are invited to take part in collective projects, experiment with design approaches and discover the fundamentals of project creation (ideation, prototyping, communication, funding).

## Objectives

- Foster student autonomy and creativity.
- Provide methods for turning an idea into a concrete project.
- Connect students with academic tutors and professional partners.

## Support

The programme offers:
- Practical workshops on entrepreneurship and design thinking.
- Mentoring by tutors (lecturers, researchers and professionals).
- Pitch sessions and feedback sessions with entrepreneurs and alumni.

## Examples of activities

- Social and solidarity project: teamwork addressing a real identified need in the local area.
- Incubators / student competitions: information and guidance for promising projects.

For any questions about entrepreneurship support, contact the academic coordination team.
