# Visits and meetings with laboratories

The CPES regularly organises laboratory visits and meetings with partner research teams from Université Paris-Saclay, ENS Paris-Saclay, Institut Polytechnique de Paris, HEC Paris, and other actors of the scientific and entrepreneurial ecosystem in the Paris region. These immersions allow students to confront academic knowledge with reality on the ground, discover research careers and identify pathways for the rest of their studies.

## Examples of visits

- Research centres in data science and artificial intelligence.
- Laboratories in life sciences, biology and public health.
- Technical platforms and high-performance computing centres.
- Centre for Nanoscience and Nanotechnology (C2N), on the Paris-Saclay campus.
- Station F, one of the largest startup campuses in the world, in Paris.

## Visit format

Visits are complemented by presentations of ongoing research projects, thematic conferences and direct Q&A sessions with researchers, engineers or entrepreneurs. These exchanges offer a concrete perspective on the scientific, technological and entrepreneurial opportunities available after the CPES.
