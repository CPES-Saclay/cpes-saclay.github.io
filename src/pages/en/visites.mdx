# Visits and meetings with laboratories

The CPES occasionally organises laboratory visits and meetings with speakers from the partner institutions and other actors of the scientific and entrepreneurial ecosystem in the Paris region. These immersions allow students to confront academic knowledge with reality on the ground, discover research careers and identify pathways for the rest of their studies.

## Examples of visits or talks

- Research centres
- Laboratories in life sciences, biology and public health
- Centre for Nanoscience and Nanotechnology (C2N), on the Paris-Saclay campus.
- Station F, one of the largest startup campuses in the world, in Paris.

## Visit format

Visits are complemented by presentations of ongoing research projects, thematic conferences and direct Q&A sessions with researchers, engineers or entrepreneurs. These exchanges offer a concrete perspective on the scientific, technological and entrepreneurial opportunities available after the CPES.
