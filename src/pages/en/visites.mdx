# Laboratory visits and meetings with research teams

The CPES regularly organises laboratory visits and meetings with research teams from partner institutions (Université Paris-Saclay, ENS Paris-Saclay, Institut Polytechnique de Paris, HEC, etc.). These visits allow students to discover research careers and explore topics covered in class in greater depth.

## Examples of visits

- Research centres in data science and AI.
- Laboratories in life sciences and public health.
- Technical platforms and computing centres.

These visits are complemented by presentations of ongoing projects and Q&A sessions with researchers.
