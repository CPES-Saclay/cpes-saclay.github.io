# Languages, sport and cultural workshops

The CPES offers a personal development strand built around three areas: language learning, sports activities and cultural workshops, notably in partnership with Université Paris-Saclay. These activities aim to develop transferable skills, intercultural awareness and cohort cohesion.

## Languages

- Reinforced English courses focused on scientific and academic terminology, taught in half-groups within the school.
- Khôlles in English and project presentations in English, preparing students for the requirements of international programmes and grandes écoles.
- Intercultural exchange workshops and preparation for international mobility.
- Access to a wide catalogue of languages through Université Paris-Saclay, at all levels: Spanish, German, Chinese, Arabic, Japanese, etc.

## Sport

- Access to the sports facilities of Université Paris-Saclay.
- Local sports clubs and competitions (basketball, football, badminton, swimming, etc.).
- Supervised practice encouraging regularity and physical balance, as a complement to the intensity of the programme.

## Cultural workshops

- Workshops in a variety of fields: music, theatre, cinema, visual arts, etc.
- Access to the campus cultural programme.
- Opportunity to take part in collective interdisciplinary projects with an artistic or civic dimension.

These activities are open to all CPES students and are an essential way of balancing the academic demands of the programme with a rich and structured student life.
