# Languages, sport and cultural workshops

The CPES offers a personal development strand that includes language learning, sports activities and cultural workshops. These activities aim to develop transferable skills, intercultural awareness and group cohesion.

## Languages

- Reinforced English courses covering scientific terminology.
- Intercultural exchange workshops and preparation for international mobility.

## Sport and culture

- Sports clubs and local competitions (e.g. basketball, football).
- Cultural workshops: music, theatre, cinema, etc. (e.g. Lia, member of the orchestra).

## Student testimonials

- Lia: participation in the orchestra and music workshops.
- Simon: member of a sports team and winner of a local tournament.

These activities are open to all students and promote a balance between academic rigour and student life.
