# Admission

The CPES Paris-Saclay admits 48 students each year through the **Parcoursup** platform. Selection is national and based on the excellence of the academic record, the quality of the study plan and the motivation of the applicant.

## Requirements

Applicants must hold a general baccalaureate with the **mathematics** specialisation and a second specialisation from among: SES, SVT, Physics-Chemistry, History-Geography Geopolitics and Political Sciences, Engineering Sciences or Computer Science (NSI).

A genuine interest in interdisciplinary approaches and curiosity about societal issues are also required.

For international students: the ability to express and argue in French (oral and written) is required.

### Selection criteria grid

The programme places particular importance on a **carefully written and personal study plan**. Participation in equal opportunities schemes (cordées de la réussite) is also taken into account.

#### Advice for applicants

A carefully written and personal study plan is necessary and appreciated, given the interdisciplinary nature of the programme and the diversity of its partners.

##### Application conditions for international students

If you are a foreign national, do not hold a French baccalaureate, are not a citizen of the European Union (EU), the European Economic Area (EEA), Switzerland, Monaco or Andorra, and reside in a country with a Campus France office running the **Études en France** procedure: you must apply through [campusfrance.org](https://www.campusfrance.org) and not through Parcoursup.
