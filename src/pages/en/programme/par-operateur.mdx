# Detailed educational programme by teaching institution

The institutions delivering teaching at the CPES Paris-Saclay are ENS Paris-Saclay, HEC Paris, Institut Polytechnique de Paris, the Lycée International de Palaiseau Paris-Saclay and Université Paris-Saclay. Each operator makes a specific contribution to the educational programme, with courses and projects that reflect their expertise and field of excellence.

<Disclaimer>The programme is regularly updated to adapt.</Disclaimer>

<Disclaimer>Only the courses delivered by each operator are presented below; cross-cutting activities such as projects, conferences, etc. are not included.</Disclaimer>
