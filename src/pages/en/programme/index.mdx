## Courses

Courses are delivered at the heart of the Paris-Saclay campus by lecturers or lecturer-researchers from the Lycée International de Palaiseau Paris-Saclay, Université Paris-Saclay, ENS Paris-Saclay, Institut Polytechnique de Paris and HEC Paris.

The programme is organised into blocks spread over three years, with a progressive increase in teaching carried out by the higher education and research institutions (ESR).
